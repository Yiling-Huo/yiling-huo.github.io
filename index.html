About me

Having started my PhD journey at UCL, the world of research feels both exciting and new. While these pages might seem a bit sparse for now, I'm dedicated to filling them with my work!

My journey to research is only possible with the support of my dear family, my friends, and my very supportive supervisors.

I am currently a PhD student at UCL Linguistics. I work with my supervisor Dr. Wing-Yee Chow on prediction during language comprehension. My research delves into the role of phonological information in sentence-level prediction, as well as what factors may enhance or inhibit prediction during language comprehension.

Aside from my passion for research and teaching, I find joy in hobbies like video games, cooking, and traveling.

You can explore some of my workflow and scripts on my tutorials and resources pages.
