About me

Currently pursuing a PhD in Psycholinguistics at UCL, I'm honing my skills to transform research and data into impactful insights. I am a skilled researcher, data scientist, and scientific communicator.

My journey is only possible with the support of my dear family, my friends, and my very supportive supervisors.

I am currently a PhD student at UCL Linguistics. I work with my supervisor Dr. Wing-Yee Chow on prediction during language comprehension. My research delves into the role of phonological information in lexical prediction, as well as what factors may enhance or inhibit prediction during language comprehension.

Aside from my passion for research and teaching, I find joy in hobbies like photography, video games, cooking, and traveling.
